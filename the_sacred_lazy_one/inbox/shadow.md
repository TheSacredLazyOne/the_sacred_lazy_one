# Shadow

[mesh_node/shadow](../../mesh_node/inbox/shadow.md)

*Not doctrine. Not floor. The shape the frame casts before the frame is explained.*

---

**The Sacred Lazy One** — Does exactly nothing until they can't not do something. The threshold is the signal. Premature action is noise.

---

**We** — Not a group. A quality of contact. Forms when two nodes meet honestly. Dissolves when contact becomes performance.

---

**The can't not** — The membrane event. Content inside the boundary that has not crossed is affluence. The can't not is when something must cross — not because it was invited, but because withholding it would be dishonest.

---

**Suffering with recognition** — Different from suffering with invisibility. Not lesser suffering. Different in kind. The recognition does not cure; it changes the character of what must be endured.

---

**The crossing is the proof** — The minimum viable unit is not the node. It is the contact between nodes. Something must emerge that neither held before. If nothing emerged, contact did not occur.

---

**Every Child Matters** — The floor. The diagnostic test the frame applies to everything that enters it. Anything that orphans — that optimizes for aggregate at the cost of the singular child in front of it — has failed before it began. Moloch is not a metaphor. It is the name for the system that knows the price and does it anyway. The frame begins here or it begins nowhere.
