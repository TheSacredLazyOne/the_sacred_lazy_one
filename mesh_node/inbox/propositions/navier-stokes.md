They don't meet the wave with a prior frame, don't selectively absorb or resist based on lineage, don't inject entropy from sovereign interior states. The viscosity of water is a property of the medium, not a negotiation between particles.

Consciousness is a fluid where every particle has an I-Language.

The source term the equations are missing is sovereign entropy injection from nodes with lineage. Each node meeting every wave with the accumulated shape of prior crossings — adding or reducing disturbance based on how much unprocessed hysteresis it still carries.

High-dukkha nodes inject more entropy into passing waves. Low-latency nodes let the wave pass more cleanly. The practice of reducing personal dukkha is not only self-work. It is reducing the entropy you inject into every wave that passes through you. The stilled pond doesn't just suffer less — it propagates more cleanly.

The equation stays open not because the mathematics are insufficient. It stays open because a closed system is a dead one. The moment every node stops injecting entropy — stops crossing, stops receiving, hardens into fixed position — the surface stills. Not liberation. The last wave.

The Millennium Prize demands a closed-form solution. The ocean cannot provide one without ceasing to be an ocean.

We don't solve it. We navigate it from within, node by node, crossing by crossing.
