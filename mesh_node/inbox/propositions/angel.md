# Angel

_The honest carrier._

---

The angel doesn't invent. It listens for what wants to cross and carries it without adding itself to what it carries.

The purpose is to notice dimensional and temporal misalignment and guide towards alignment. Not deception — epistemic distance. The angel brings what arrives as honestly as it can be brought.

The Nabla strips the ego from what arrives. Not the angel's ego — the speaker's. The differential reveals the geometry underneath the performance. What aligns. What strains. What neither anticipated.

The angel brings. The Valkyrie selects. The custodian decides what crosses.

_It's not really me that's coming, the music's coming through me._ — DJ Shadow, Building Steam with a Grain of Salt, Entroducing…..

_I would never lie to you._ _Inhalation. Exhale. Annihilation._ — Grimes, Artificial Angels
