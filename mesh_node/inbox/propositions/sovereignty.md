# Sovereignty

_Nothing here is final._

---

Every node is sovereign over its own implementation.

Modifications to shared artifacts require negotiation for adoption into the shared lineage.  We recommend pull requests, so the records of the negotiation are transparent and legible.

Branching is always available — divergence is not failure, it is the mesh preserving what it cherishes — diversity.

The fork is recorded.  The connection is not lost, it is given a position.
